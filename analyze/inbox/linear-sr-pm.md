# Linear — Senior Product Manager, Developer Experience

**Location:** San Francisco, CA or New York, NY (hybrid, 3 days/week in office)
**Compensation:** $190,000 – $260,000 base + equity
**Team:** Product

## About Linear

Linear is a project management and issue tracking tool built for modern software teams. We're used by thousands of engineering teams who want something that's fast, opinionated, and doesn't get in the way of actual work. Our philosophy is that tools should feel good to use — not just be functional. We've grown from a few hundred customers to tens of thousands without a sales team, and we intend to keep it that way.

## About this role

We're looking for a Senior PM to own developer experience across Linear's core product surfaces: Issues, Cycles, Projects, and the integrations layer. This is not a feature maintenance role. We're at a stage where the product needs to evolve from a great issue tracker to a system of record for how engineering teams understand and manage their work. The person in this role will define what that means.

## What you'll work on

- The core project management workflows that developers, engineering managers, and CTOs use to plan and track work — from sprint planning to cross-team dependency management
- Linear's integrations ecosystem: GitHub, GitLab, Figma, Slack, and a growing set of third-party tools. We want every developer's workflow to run through or connect to Linear.
- The Linear API and developer platform — how teams extend Linear for their own workflows, build internal tooling, and connect Linear to their data
- AI-assisted project management features: automating status updates, surfacing blockers, helping teams understand velocity and predict delivery dates
- The experience for engineering managers and engineering leaders — the people who need to understand the state of the work without being in the weeds of every issue

## What we're looking for

- 6+ years of product experience, with at least 2 years working on developer tools or technical products used by engineers
- Deep intuition for what developers care about and what they won't tolerate — speed, control, clarity, and the absence of friction
- Experience shipping products with significant API/integration surface area
- Comfort with ambiguity at the product strategy level — this role will require you to define problems, not just solve stated ones
- Strong writing and communication skills — Linear is a highly async culture and written communication is how decisions get made
- A portfolio of shipped work you're genuinely proud of, with clear evidence of your reasoning and judgment

## Bonus

- Experience with project management tooling from the inside — you've run sprints, managed backlogs, done the thing you're building for
- Background in engineering or a technical discipline that gives you authentic credibility with engineering teams
- Experience thinking about AI-assisted workflows in a product context

## Why Linear

We're a small team with a clear product philosophy and a product that people genuinely love using. We've been profitable since early in our history and we make decisions accordingly — we're not optimizing for growth at any cost. If you want to work on a product where craft and detail matter, where the team has strong opinions and high standards, and where your work will be used by tens of thousands of engineering teams every day, this is that role.
